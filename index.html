Mod Tracker
Transparency is not a word in Moderators' vocabulary. They like to be in shadows so that wrong actions get unnoticed.

Not anymore. Here it is a public tool so that you can make your own arguments based on live data of their activity in Transformice.

A score point is registered every 5 minutes; However it does not mean they were online for 5 minutes, but for within a range from 1 second to 5 minutes.
To calculate the time in minutes, you may use:
 max => (score * 5);
 min => score / 100;
